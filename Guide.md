# 📖 User Guide: How to use mykey

### 1️⃣ The Master Password
When you start the app, you'll see a prompt. This is the "Key" to your house. 
- **User Tip**: Make it a "Passphrase" (e.g., `TheBlueDogJumped@2024`) instead of a password. It's harder for computers to crack but easier for you to remember.

### 2️⃣ Adding a Service
1. Type the name (e.g., `Netflix`).
2. Click **Generate & Save**. 
3. The app handles the AES-256-GCM encryption in the background.

### 3️⃣ Recovery Mode
If you forget your password, click the red **Emergency** button. 
Enter your 12-word phrase to "Force Unlock" the vault.
