# mykey User Guide
---
## Step 1: Initialization
- Enter a **Master Password**. 
- *Warning*: Losing this means losing access to your vault forever.
---
## Step 2: Generating Passwords
- Enter a service (e.g., 'GitHub').
- Click **Generate & Save**. 
- The vault encrypts this instantly using AES-256-GCM.
---
## Step 3: Security Hygiene
- Use the **Copy** button to avoid typing.
- The clipboard will self-destruct in **60 seconds**.
